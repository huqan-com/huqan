# AXIOM Pitch v0

## One Line

AXIOM is a local-first verification layer that tells you what is known, unknown, contradicted, or risky before you act.

## Short Pitch

AXIOM sits around models, tools, and memory. It does not try to be another model. It judges claims against graph-backed evidence, attaches receipts to trust decisions, and keeps mutating actions behind deterministic gates.

## What To Say

- `AXIOM verifies claims before they become decisions.`
- `Every serious answer should come with a receipt.`
- `Unsupported knowledge does not become trusted memory.`
- `The trust boundary stays local and auditable.`

## What Not To Say

- `guarantees truth`
- `eliminates hallucinations`
- `fully autonomous agent control plane`
- `production-scale enterprise governance`
- `full NLP engine`

## Positioning Paragraph

AXIOM is for builders who need a deterministic trust layer around AI-assisted workflows. It is useful when the cost of a wrong answer is higher than the cost of a slower, provable answer.
